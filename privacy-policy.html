Privacy Policy

PostKeeperFit Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 15, 2026

PostKeeperFit helps users save, organize, and manage post ideas and shared links. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles, how it is used, and what choices users have.

Information Stored on Your Device

PostKeeperFit stores post entries, notes, archive status, and related preview images locally on the user’s device and in the app group storage used by the app and its share extension.

Information Sent to Third Parties

PostKeeperFit uses RevenueCat to support subscriptions, restore purchases, and determine premium access status. For this purpose, the app may send a generated app-specific user identifier and purchase-related information to RevenueCat.

The app does not use this identifier for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Link Preview Data

When a saved post includes a web link, the app may request preview metadata, such as a title or preview image, in order to show a richer card inside the app. This request is made only for links the user has chosen to save or share into the app.

What We Do Not Collect for Advertising

  • We do not use personal data for third-party advertising.
  • We do not sell user data.
  • We do not use user identifiers for tracking across apps or websites.
  • We do not collect precise or coarse location data.

Analytics

PostKeeperFit does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs for user behavior profiling, based on the current version of the app.

Data Retention

Locally stored posts and notes remain on the device until the user deletes them or removes the app. Purchase and subscription records handled through Apple and RevenueCat are retained according to those providers’ own policies.

Children's Privacy

PostKeeperFit is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

  • You can delete saved content inside the app.
  • You can choose not to purchase a subscription.
  • You can avoid saving links if you do not want preview metadata to be fetched.

Third-Party Services

PostKeeperFit may rely on third-party services that have their own privacy policies, including:

  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If material changes are made, the updated version will be posted with a new effective date.
