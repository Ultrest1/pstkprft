Help for PostKeeperFit users

If you need help with PostKeeperFit, this page explains the fastest way to get support and what information to include in your message.

What We Can Help With

  • Subscription and premium access issues
  • Restore purchases not working
  • Problems saving or viewing posts
  • Share extension issues
  • Bug reports and general feedback

Before You Contact Support

  • Make sure you are using the latest version of the app.
  • Restart the app and try the action again.
  • If the issue is related to purchases, try the restore purchases option in the app.

Helpful Details to Include

  • Screenshots, if available

Response Time

Support requests are typically answered as soon as possible. Response times may vary depending on volume and time zone.
